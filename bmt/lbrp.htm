The Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP)


1 - Stand in the middle of your sacred space. Face east . Take a few deep breath and relax.
2 -Using the first two fingers and thumb as if you are holding a small ball of blue light, point to your forehead and vibrate the word

Ah-Toi.

(For Thou)
3 - Trace this blue light down to the groin. Vibrate the word

Malkuth

(Art the Kingdom)
4 - Trace the light to your right shoulder. Vibrate the word

Ve-Geburah

(And the Power)
5 - Trace the light to your left shoulder. Vibrate the word

Ve-Gedulah

(And the Glory)
6 -Extend arms out as if encompasing everything then bring your hands together at your chest as if praying and vibrate

La-Olam O-men

(Forever. Amen)
7 - With the index and middle fingers of your right hand, draw a pentagram in the air in front of you starting from lower left corner and continue clockwise. It should be color of blue flame. When complete point fingers or athame at center of pentagram and vibrate from depths of being

Ihoah.


8 - Pivot 90 degrees clockwise. As you do so , use your right hand to draw a line in the air from the center of the first pentagram to the center of the area you are going to use to draw another pentagram. Draw another blue pentagram in the air in the same way you drew the first one. Point to the center of it and vibrate

Adonai


9 - Repeat this procedure turning 90 degrees clockwise. After you make the pentagram, point to the center and vibrate

Eheieh

(pronounced E-heee-h and should sound like ocean waves)
10 - Repeat again, only this time vibrate

AGLA.

(pronounced Ah-gla) Draw a line from this pentagram to the first to the first one as you again pivot 90 degrees. You should again be facing east.
11 Put your arms directly in front of you such that they form a cross. The left arm is the vertical beam and the right is the horizontal. The hands should be in loose fists.
Invoke the Archangels. Be sure to visualize the banner of the west after invoking each Archangel to test it and do not continue until doing so. Do not assume any trappings or costumes. Only the color is important. The archangels should be in simple colored robes. Raphael is in yellow. Gabriel is in blue. Michael (pronounced Me-ka-el) is in red. Auriel is in green. The truer the color, the stronger is your connection with the archangel.
Say:

Before me, Raphael
Behind me, Gabriel
On my right hand, Michael
And on my left hand, Auriel

Visualize a hexagram in the SE corner. The upper triangle should be red and the lower should be blue. It is encased by an upright white pillar. Say:

Around me blaze the pentagrams in the column shines the six rayed star

12 - repeat steps 2 - 6.
When completed and you feel centered raise right hand straight up and with first two fingers pull in the spirit from above and throw it into the SE corner. Trace it around the circle clockwise back to the point where you started. It should appear as a blue flame.
